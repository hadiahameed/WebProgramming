My Education

University of Engineering & Technology (UET), Lahore, Pakistan

My favorite class in UET was "Digital Signal Processing" (DSP) by Prof. Dr. Asim Loan. He was a great teacher and was notorious for failing his students if they gave anything less than their very best. I loved how he made us work hard.

My most memorable memory from UET is when I conducted the first STEM Women's conference titled "STEM for All: Claiming an Education". In this conference, we invited inspiring women from different parts of the country to visit UET and share their stories.

Kinnaird College for Women University (KC), Lahore, Pakistan

At KC, my favorite class was "Chemistry Lab" because of all the fun science adventures and (mis)adventures we did in it.

My most memorable memory from KC is sitting under a gigantic Banyan tree, which everyone called "The Big Tree" and reading novels on lazy summer afternoons.

Beaconhouse School System (BSS), Lahore, Pakistan

At BSS, my favorite class was "Urdu" because our teacher used to tell us wonderful stories.

My most memorable memory from BSS is being part of the Debate Team.
